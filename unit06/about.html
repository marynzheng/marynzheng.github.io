Mary N. Zheng

Mary is a fourth-year undergraduate student at the University of California, Berkeley.

She studies Political Economy with a concentration in Chinese Economy and Society; but true to her interdisciplinary college major, Mary also has a heart for photo-/videography, art and design, old literature, the French language, hiking, music making, and birds.

On any given day, Mary can usually be found helping out with her church in Berkeley, creating graphics as a part of Taulia's marketing team in San Francisco, happily slurping avocado smoothies, or reading books (but actually taking naps) on Memorial Glade.

After she graduates in May 2015, Mary hopes to work in graphic design, web development, or UI/UX design.
